Hajj Telecom COW Power & Cooling Risk Dashboard
System Specification for Dashboard Development
Project Title

Hajj 1447 COW Sites Power & Cooling Risk Monitoring Dashboard

1. Project Overview

This project aims to develop an interactive operational dashboard to evaluate power, cooling, rectifier, and battery risks for telecom Cell on Wheels (COW) GSM sites deployed during Hajj 1447.

The system will monitor 94 telecom sites operated by stc, supporting pilgrims across several locations including:

Jeddah Islamic Port
Jeddah–Makkah Highway
Makkah
Masjid al‑Haram
Mina
Muzdalifah
Arafat

These sites are deployed by Nokia telecom infrastructure.

The dashboard must analyze multiple operational scenarios under extreme environmental conditions (46°C) to prevent telecom service interruptions during peak pilgrimage traffic.

2. System Objective

The dashboard should:

Evaluate technical risk scenarios for each telecom site.
Quantify power supply margins.
Detect cooling insufficiency risks.
Identify battery autonomy limitations.
Provide overall site risk classification.
Recommend technician deployment strategy.
Display visual monitoring for all 94 sites.

9. Risk Evaluation Categories

Each scenario must calculate four types of risk.

1 Power Supply Risk

Condition:

Available generator power
minus
Total load demand

If negative

Power risk exists

2 Rectifier Supply Risk

Rectifier capacity
minus
Telecom power load

3 Battery Supply Risk

Battery maximum discharge power
must support telecom load

Battery backup time must be sufficient

4 Cooling Supply Risk

Total cooling capacity
minus
telecom heat dissipation

If cooling capacity is insufficient

Cooling risk exists
